Background information:

The internationalisation in many companies and projects requires a better understanding of working conditions in different cultures to enable an enhanced integration of employees in foreign working environments.

We now know that the motivation, well-being and performance of employees are increasing if the individual needs are considered in the workplace design process. The challenge therefore is how to bring several individual needs into a multinational workplace as a factor of promoting integration and well-being of every employee equally.

Our goal is the identification of different (working) types and success factors on a global scale. On this basis, recommendations and suggestions for a workspace design of multinational working environments are derived.

By participating in our survey of about 5 minutes you can help us explore complex interdependencies and make an important contribution to create equal working conditions for employees from all cultural backgrounds.
